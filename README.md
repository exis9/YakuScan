Have you ever given up trying to play a game in a foreign language?

This is a tool that can extract text from any selected area, image, or window on your screen.

It can also integrate with local AI or dictionary websites, allowing you to look up word meanings, generate example sentences, and translate text. It even supports text-to-speech, making it a reliable companion when playing games in foreign languages.

Even though setting up local AI is usually very complex, this tool includes a fully automatic setup button, making it so easy that you can complete everything with just a single click.

You can choose from various AI options depending on your PC's performance, and it can even run on a laptop, at least to some extent.
